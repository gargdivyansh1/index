Key Problems

· Accrued Student Loans

Society has put a lot of pressure on young adults to attend expensive universities and then compete for limited job opportunities, in the process of pursuing your education, you get in debt to fund your studies. After graduation, you are already in deep education debt before landing your first well-paying job.br

Solution

· You should borrow wisely{take what you need} · Work while on campus and start paying debt while in school · Apply for as many bursaries as possible · If you are already working, consider increasing your income, then pay the loan twice a month.

· Inexperience among the youth who are spoon-fed

Living with parents or guardians who spoiled you, cushions you into a comfort zone. So when you decide to live on your own, you will often face trouble managing money. And this can be scary.

Solution

· Following a parent's guidance or asking for experienced persons to help you navigate through any challenges you come across. · Read personal finance books and blogs then implement. · Always ask for help.

· Lack of a financial plan

If you are employed or in the university, you tend to live only for today or for the current semester or job. You become short-sighted and realize too late that you should have started saving and budgeting earlier.

Solution

· When you fail to plan, you get into debt then end up regretting and wishing you had learned to plan sooner. If you don’t plan for your money, someone else will plan for it.
